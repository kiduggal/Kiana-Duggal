Redesigning Everyday Light Switches for Improved Usability

In this project, we conducted in-person interviews with college students to understand how different light switch designs affect user interactions and identify common errors. Using our findings, we analyzed how factors like consistency, affordances, and spatial mapping influence usability and user confidence. Based on these insights, we developed two lo-fi redesigns that balance functionality, simplicity, and clear visual cues to reduce errors and create a more intuitive everyday experience.
